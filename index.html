🔍 Industrial Audits

  • Electrical Audit: Identifies energy losses, safety risks, and inefficiencies in your electrical systems.
  • Water Audit: Evaluates water usage to detect leaks, reduce wastage, and optimize consumption.
  • Steam Audit: Analyzes steam systems to improve efficiency, reduce energy costs, and enhance safety.

⚙️ Automation Solutions

  • Rice Dryer Automation: Real-time logging, batch control, and performance optimization.
